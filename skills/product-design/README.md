# product-design

> Research, design, and shape product directions for personal products.

## What it does

`product-design` helps decide whether an idea is worth doing, how a feature
should land in the next version, and what similar products suggest without
turning the work into enterprise process. It researches existing product
patterns when external examples matter, then translates those observations into
right-sized recommendations, product directions, or version plans.

## When it triggers

- "这个功能要不要做", "下一版怎么迭代", "这个想法怎么落地"
- "帮我看看类似产品", "别人怎么做", "先调研一下", "不要凭空想"
- Personal product positioning, scope, feature tradeoff, or version planning
- Research-driven product advice for a solo or small personal product
